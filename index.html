Tigers ROAR!

Hansen Elementary School

We are well into our second year as Hansen Tigers. We look forward to continuing to build our programs, school culture, and Tiger Community. Hansen Students work hard and it shows. Last year our students competed in Math, Science, Music, Mock Trial and HOSA/JUMP events and put Hansen on the map as far south as Florida and Texas. We are proud of all that our teams and individual competitors have accomplished. I am confident we will continue to shine this year! As students work toward mastery of the Common Core State Standards through rigorous content, we also know how important it is to foster creativity, good character, and a positive sense of self. Enrichment, extension, and exploration are critical to student success at school and in life. It is my goal that every student reach his or her true potential at Hansen and beyond.
